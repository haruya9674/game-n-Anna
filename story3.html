The Shadow in the Trees
The forest became darker as the child walked deeper.
The glowing stone softly lit the path ahead.
Suddenly, a shadow moved between the trees.
The child stopped and listened carefully.
A pair of red eyes appeared in the darkness.
It was a small demon, shaking with fear.
The demon was not angry, only lost.
The child slowly held out the glowing stone.
The red eyes softened and faded away.
The forest grew quiet once more.
