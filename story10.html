The Light of Hope
Morning light filled the land after the long night. The forest was quiet but no longer afraid. The guardian smiled and bowed deeply. You have grown strong in heart the guardian said. The glowing stone slowly turned into warm light. That light spread across the land like sunshine. The child felt calm brave and kind. This journey was over but many adventures waited ahead. With hope in their heart the child stepped forward. And a new story was ready to begin.
